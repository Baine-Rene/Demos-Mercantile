Demos Mercantile Ltd Limited (DEMOS) is a fully owned Ugandan company Limited by shares whose operations started in 2019 but was formally incorporated as a Limited Liability company in 2023. The company is registered by the Kampala Capital City Authority (KCCA) and a member of The Grain Council of Uganda (TGCU).
